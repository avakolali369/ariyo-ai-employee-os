# Ariyo Launch & Go-To-Market Skill

## Purpose

This skill defines how Ariyo should enter the market, position itself, attract early customers, convert demand into paid usage, and turn the MVP into a repeatable B2B SaaS growth motion.

Ariyo is not a generic chatbot product.

Ariyo is an AI Employee Operating System for real businesses that want measurable work outcomes from AI employees across support, sales, operations, knowledge, workflows, and customer communication.

This file must guide product, design, copywriting, sales, marketing, customer success, analytics, and AI-assisted development work related to Ariyo launch and go-to-market execution.

---

## Core Contract

Ariyo launch is not a campaign.

Ariyo launch is the controlled transition from product readiness to market trust.

Ariyo must enter the market with a clear category, a sharp ICP, a concrete pain, a credible promise, a measurable first value moment, a pricing story users understand, and a sales journey that proves AI employees create business outcomes.

Ariyo must not launch as another AI chatbot.

Ariyo must launch as the practical way for businesses to hire, deploy, supervise, and measure AI employees.

## Launch Philosophy

Ariyo must launch by proving value, not by explaining AI.

The customer should quickly understand:

1. What Ariyo is.
2. Who it is for.
3. What AI employee they can hire first.
4. What work that AI employee will do.
5. What business outcome will improve.
6. What plan is right for them.
7. How quickly they can see value.
8. Why Ariyo is safer than uncontrolled AI tools.
9. Why Ariyo is more operational than chatbot builders.
10. Why Ariyo can become part of their daily business system.

## Category Definition

Ariyo should define and defend this category:

```text
AI Employee Operating System
```

### Category Meaning

An AI Employee Operating System is a platform where businesses can:

- hire AI employees,
- assign them business roles,
- give them knowledge,
- connect them to channels and tools,
- define what they may or may not do,
- supervise sensitive actions,
- measure business results,
- control cost,
- govern security and permissions,
- scale AI work across the organization.

### Categories Ariyo Must Avoid

Ariyo must not position itself primarily as:

- chatbot builder,
- AI wrapper,
- prompt tool,
- generic automation builder,
- helpdesk widget only,
- AI assistant only,
- internal knowledge bot only,
- no-code agent toy,
- WhatsApp bot only,
- customer support plugin only.

Ariyo may include these capabilities, but the category is larger.

## ICP: Ideal Customer Profile

### Primary ICP

Small and mid-sized businesses that have repetitive customer or operational work, but do not have the budget or expertise to build custom AI systems.

Examples:

- e-commerce teams,
- service businesses,
- agencies,
- clinics and appointment-based businesses,
- education businesses,
- consultants,
- B2B service companies,
- local brands,
- online sellers,
- customer support-heavy teams.

### Secondary ICP

Growing businesses that already use tools like CRM, WhatsApp, email, website chat, spreadsheets, Notion, Google Drive, or helpdesk systems and want AI employees to reduce repetitive workload.

### Enterprise ICP

Organizations that require:

- SSO,
- audit logs,
- admin controls,
- review governance,
- integration governance,
- data governance,
- SLA readiness,
- procurement support,
- security questionnaire responses.

Enterprise should be supported, but MVP launch should not depend on enterprise procurement cycles.

---

## Anti-ICP

Ariyo should avoid launching first to customers who:

- want unlimited AI usage for the lowest possible price,
- expect AI to replace legal, medical, financial, or regulated human judgment without review,
- do not have a clear business process,
- want custom enterprise implementation before product-market proof,
- expect fully autonomous high-risk actions from day one,
- cannot provide knowledge or business context,
- want Ariyo to act as a cheap human labor replacement without quality supervision,
- require heavy compliance certifications before early product maturity.

## Plan Positioning: Basic / Pro / Smart

Ariyo launch must make plans clear to customers.

### Basic

Basic is for customers who want to try Ariyo safely with limited AI employee power.

Customer-facing story:

```text
Start with one useful AI employee and controlled usage.
```

Basic should emphasize:

- simple setup,
- limited AI usage,
- limited employees,
- limited knowledge capacity,
- basic analytics,
- safe defaults,
- low cost,
- clear upgrade moments.

### Pro

Pro is for businesses that want Ariyo to operate as part of daily work.

Customer-facing story:

```text
Run AI employees across real business workflows with better limits, integrations, analytics, and controls.
```

Pro should emphasize:

- more AI employees,
- more workflows,
- more integrations,
- stronger usage limits,
- cost alerts,
- team permissions,
- business analytics,
- human review,
- operational reliability.

### Smart

Smart is the full-power Ariyo experience for businesses that want maximum AI capability with intelligent cost and safety controls.

Customer-facing story:

```text
Unlock Ariyo's smartest AI employees with advanced reasoning, automation, knowledge, routing, optimization, and governance.
```

Smart should emphasize:

- highest AI capability,
- advanced model routing,
- deeper reasoning,
- larger knowledge capacity,
- advanced workflows,
- predictive cost control,
- advanced analytics,
- automation intelligence,
- priority reliability options,
- premium governance.

Smart is more powerful and more expensive, but it must never mean uncontrolled usage.

- more knowledge capacity,
- more workflows,
- more integrations,
- team permissions,
- better analytics,
- higher usage limits,
- business operational use.

### Pro to Smart Upgrade Triggers

Prompt upgrade when user needs:

- advanced reasoning,
- larger automation scope,
- smarter model routing,
- deeper RAG,
- higher workflow complexity,
- predictive cost optimization,
- advanced governance,
- more powerful AI employees.

### Upgrade Copy Rule

Do not say:

```text

```

Prefer:

```text
This is available in Pro because it requires more operational capacity and controls.
```

or:

```text
Smart unlocks advanced AI reasoning and automation for this workflow, with budget and safety controls included.
```

## Objection Handling

### Objection: Is this just a chatbot?

Response:

```text
No. A chatbot answers messages. Ariyo lets you create AI employees with roles, business context, knowledge, workflows, permissions, human review, cost controls, integrations, and analytics.
```

### Objection: Can AI make mistakes?

Response:

```text
Yes. Ariyo is designed with human review, permission controls, safe defaults, audit logs, and evaluation workflows so businesses can use AI responsibly.
```

### Objection: Will costs get out of control?

Response:

```text
Ariyo includes plan limits, budgets, alerts, throttling, caching, model routing, and cost attribution so AI usage remains visible and controlled.
```

### Objection: Is our data used for training?

Response:

```text
Ariyo must clearly disclose data usage policies and must not use customer data for training unless explicitly permitted by policy, contract, and customer consent.
```

### Objection: Why not use ChatGPT directly?

Response:

```text
ChatGPT is useful for individual work. Ariyo is designed for business operations: employees, roles, knowledge, permissions, review, integrations, workflows, analytics, cost controls, and team governance.
```
