# Ariyo Content Marketing Engine Skill

## 1. Purpose

This skill defines Ariyo's content marketing operating system.

Ariyo content marketing exists to educate the market, create category demand, build trust, generate qualified leads, support sales conversations, increase product activation, and turn the idea of AI employees into a clear, credible, practical business movement.

Ariyo must not publish generic AI content.

Ariyo must publish content that helps business owners, operators, support leaders, sales leaders, founders, and enterprise buyers understand how to hire, train, supervise, govern, measure, and scale AI employees safely.

## 2. Core Contract

Ariyo content marketing is not posting about AI trends.

Ariyo content marketing is the repeatable system that turns product strategy, customer pain, category positioning, proof, education, SEO, social distribution, sales enablement, and customer success into a measurable acquisition engine.

Every Ariyo content asset must answer one of these questions:

1. Why should a business care about AI employees now?
2. What business problem does an AI employee solve?
3. How does Ariyo make AI employees useful, safe, measurable, and affordable?
4. Why is Ariyo different from chatbots, automation tools, and generic AI agents?
5. What should the reader do next?

If content does not educate, build trust, generate intent, reduce objections, create proof, or support conversion, it should not be produced.

Ariyo's content engine must consistently build this category narrative:

## 16. Basic / Pro / Smart Content Messaging

Content must make the three plan levels clear without overwhelming the user.

### 16.1 Basic Content Angle

Basic should be described as:

- A safe starting point.
- Useful for trying the AI employee model.
- Best for simple tasks and first workflows.
- Controlled, low-cost, and limited.

Example messaging:

> Start with one focused AI employee and learn where AI can help your business safely.

Content topics:

- Your first AI employee.
- Basic setup checklist.
- When Basic is enough.
- How to avoid over-automation early.

### 16.2 Pro Content Angle

Pro should be described as:

- The operational plan for serious daily use.
- Best for growing teams.
- Supports more employees, workflows, knowledge, integrations, and reporting.
- Built for repeatable business operations.

Example messaging:

> Move from AI experimentation to daily AI employee operations.

Content topics:

- Scaling from one AI employee to a team.
- How Pro improves workflow control.
- When to upgrade to Pro.
- Measuring operational ROI.

### 16.3 Smart Content Angle

Smart should be described as:

- The fullest AI employee experience.
- Best for advanced automation, deeper reasoning, richer knowledge use, higher volume, and smarter optimization.
- Higher cost because it uses more intelligence and capability.
- Controlled by advanced budget, approval, monitoring, and safety systems.

Example messaging:

> Give your business the most capable Ariyo AI employees with advanced intelligence, automation, and governance.

Content topics:

- When Smart is worth the investment.
- Advanced AI employee workflows.
- Smart plan ROI.
- Predictive cost control for advanced AI.

Do not translate slogans literally if meaning weakens.
