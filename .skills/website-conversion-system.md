# Ariyo Website Conversion System Skill

## 1. Purpose

This skill defines how Ariyo must design, write, build, measure, and continuously improve its public sales website.

The Ariyo website is not a brochure.

The Ariyo website is the primary conversion system that turns qualified visitors into activated users, booked demos, trial accounts, partner leads, investor interest, and enterprise trust.

The website must combine:

- Ariyo product strategy
- Brand messaging
- Pricing and packaging clarity
- GEO / AI SEO readiness
- Trust center credibility
- Landing page conversion architecture
- UI/UX design intelligence
- Product-led onboarding
- Founder-led sales support
- Analytics and experimentation
- Accessibility, speed, security, and reliability

## 2. Core Contract

Ariyo website conversion is not about making pages look impressive.

Ariyo website conversion is the disciplined system that helps the right customer understand the category, trust the product, choose the right plan, experience value quickly, and take the next action with confidence.

Ariyo must not launch a generic AI SaaS website.

Ariyo must launch a conversion-grade website that makes one idea unmistakable:

Businesses can hire, train, supervise, govern, and measure AI employees with Ariyo.

## 5. Website Conversion Goals

The website must support the following conversion goals:

1. Visitor understands Ariyo in less than 10 seconds.
2. Visitor understands that Ariyo is not a chatbot builder.
3. Visitor sees a concrete business outcome.
4. Visitor understands Basic, Pro, and Smart clearly.
5. Visitor can book a demo quickly.
6. Visitor can start safely without sales if eligible.
7. Enterprise buyer can access trust evidence.
8. Partner can understand how to join the ecosystem.
9. Content visitor can move from education to intent.
10. AI search engines can understand, summarize, and cite Ariyo pages.

## 11. Landing Page Copy Rules

### 11.1 Headline Rules

Headlines must be concrete.

Bad:

> Supercharge your business with AI.

Better:

> Hire AI employees that answer customers, qualify leads, run workflows, and report outcomes.

### 11.2 Subheadline Rules

Subheadline must explain:

- What Ariyo does
- Who it is for
- Why it is safe
- What outcome it creates

### 11.3 Proof Rules

Every major claim must have one of:

- Product screenshot
- Demo flow
- Customer quote
- Metric
- Trust artifact
- Workflow example
- Security explanation
- Plan entitlement

### 11.4 No-Hype Rule

Do not say:

- Fully autonomous without limits
- Replaces your whole team
- Guaranteed AI ranking
- Zero risk
- Impossible to hack
- Unlimited AI
- Magic automation

Use:

- Governed AI employees
- Human review when needed
- Measurable outcomes
- Controlled AI cost
- Safe deployment
- Transparent usage

## 18. Performance Requirements

The website must be fast enough to sell trust.

Targets:

- Largest Contentful Paint: excellent range
- Cumulative Layout Shift: minimal
- Interaction latency: low
- Mobile-first performance
- No heavy decorative animations by default
- Lazy-load non-critical media
- Optimize images
- Avoid unnecessary client-side JavaScript

Performance cannot be sacrificed for design effects.

## 23. UI UX Pro Max Workflow for Ariyo Website Pages

When designing a new Ariyo website page, use this workflow:

1. Identify page goal.
2. Identify audience.
3. Identify primary CTA.
4. Pull copy rules from Ariyo brand messaging.
5. Pull pricing truth from pricing-packaging strategy.
6. Pull trust/legal constraints from trust and legal skills.
7. Use UI UX Pro Max to propose layout pattern, visual style, typography, and anti-patterns.
8. Convert recommendation into Ariyo design tokens and component rules.
9. Validate accessibility and performance.
10. Add analytics events.
11. Add GEO-ready answer blocks and schema.
12. Run conversion QA checklist.
