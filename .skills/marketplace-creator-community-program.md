# Ariyo Marketplace Creator Community Program Skill

## 1. Core Contract

Ariyo Marketplace Creator Community Program is not a social group for template builders.

It is the governed community, education, enablement, certification, collaboration, moderation, challenge, showcase, reputation, and growth system that helps trusted creators become better builders of AI Employee marketplace assets while protecting customers, marketplace integrity, and Ariyo's brand.

Ariyo must turn high-quality creators into a durable growth network.

Ariyo must never allow community excitement, creator popularity, influencer status, or short-term marketplace supply growth to override quality, safety, compliance, customer trust, or policy enforcement.

---

## 2. Why This Skill Exists

Ariyo's Marketplace can grow only if creators, partners, agencies, consultants, developers, operators, and domain experts can build useful templates repeatedly and safely.

A creator community should help with:

- teaching creators how to build valuable AI Employee assets;
- improving template quality before review;
- reducing rejected submissions;
- promoting trustworthy creators;
- sharing best practices without leaking customer data;
- turning expert creators into marketplace ambassadors;
- creating category-specific excellence;
- building a healthy creator ecosystem that is not dependent only on Ariyo's internal team;
- preventing creator manipulation, spam, unsafe claims, and quality degradation.

This skill defines how Ariyo should design, operate, measure, and govern that community.

## 4. Community Program Goals

The Creator Community Program must optimize for:

1. **High-quality marketplace supply**  
   More useful, safe, well-documented, and installable templates.

2. **Creator education**  
   Creators understand Ariyo standards before they submit assets.

3. **Creator retention**  
   Good creators keep building, improving, and supporting assets.

4. **Category excellence**  
   Communities form around sales, finance, support, operations, GEO, RAG, HR, legal, and vertical workflows.

5. **Trust-preserving growth**  
   Community growth must not increase unsafe templates, fake reviews, policy violations, or low-quality submissions.

6. **Customer value**  
   The community must ultimately improve customer outcomes, not just creator activity.

7. **Ecosystem intelligence**  
   Ariyo learns which workflows, industries, tools, and template categories are emerging.

## 6. Community Tiers

Ariyo should define clear community tiers.

### 6.1 Explorer

Default entry-level member.

Can:

- access public education;
- join beginner discussions;
- view documentation;
- attend open webinars;
- submit draft templates for guided feedback where available.

Cannot:

- publish directly;
- mentor others officially;
- claim certification;
- access restricted creator programs.

### 6.2 Builder

Creator who has completed onboarding and basic policy education.

Can:

- submit templates for review;
- access builder workshops;
- join category groups;
- participate in challenges;
- access template quality guidance.

### 6.3 Verified Creator

Creator who has passed identity, quality, policy, and basic trust checks.

Can:

- display Verified Creator label;
- access advanced marketplace analytics;
- participate in selected showcases;
- join private creator office hours;
- receive early builder guidance.

### 6.4 Certified Creator

Creator who has passed formal Ariyo certification.

Can:

- use certification labels where allowed;
- submit for higher-trust collections;
- participate in expert programs;
- mentor under governance;
- access advanced template benchmarking.

### 6.5 Expert Creator

High-performing creator with strong trust, outcome, quality, and support history.

Can:

- lead workshops;
- participate in expert panels;
- get priority feedback;
- co-create official templates where approved;
- participate in private beta programs.

### 6.6 Partner Creator

Creator associated with an approved partner organization.

Must follow partner operations governance.

### 6.7 Suspended Creator

Creator temporarily or permanently restricted due to policy, trust, quality, fraud, dispute, or abuse issues.

```text

  -> educated
  -> active_builder
  -> submitted_template

  -> verified
  -> certified
  -> expert
  -> inactive
  -> at_risk
  -> restricted
  -> suspended
  -> reinstated
```

Each transition must be governed by signals, rules, and audit events.
