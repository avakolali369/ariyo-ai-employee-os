# Ariyo Community & Partner Growth Skill

## Purpose

This skill defines how Ariyo builds a durable growth ecosystem through communities, referrals, affiliates, consultants, agencies, SaaS partners, implementation partners, education partners, marketplace partners, and strategic alliances.

Ariyo must not rely only on paid ads or founder-led sales.

Ariyo must create a growth system where trusted people and organizations can discover Ariyo, learn it, recommend it, implement it, sell it, extend it, and prove business outcomes for customers.

---

## Core Contract

Ariyo community and partner growth is not a loose network of promoters.

Ariyo community and partner growth is the ecosystem operating system that turns customers, consultants, agencies, creators, developers, SaaS platforms, and implementation partners into trusted distribution, education, adoption, and expansion channels.

Every partner motion must protect customer trust, product positioning, brand quality, security, pricing integrity, and measurable business outcomes.

Ariyo must build a partner ecosystem that sells confidence, not hype.

## Strategic Thesis

Ariyo is not only a SaaS product.

Ariyo is a new work category: businesses hiring, training, supervising, governing, and measuring AI employees.

New categories need education, trust, proof, implementation help, and repeated explanation.

Partners help Ariyo scale all five.

Ariyo's partner strategy must create five growth advantages:

1. **Trust leverage** — customers trust advisors, agencies, consultants, and operators they already know.
2. **Implementation leverage** — partners help customers turn Ariyo from product access into real business outcomes.
3. **Education leverage** — creators, experts, and community leaders explain the category faster than Ariyo alone.
4. **Distribution leverage** — partners bring Ariyo into existing customer relationships, newsletters, events, agencies, and SaaS ecosystems.
5. **Expansion leverage** — implementation partners identify more workflows, more AI employees, more integrations, and higher-value plans.

## Partner Growth Principles

### 1. Trust Before Volume

Ariyo must not recruit partners only to increase referral count.

Partner quality matters more than partner quantity.

Bad partners create wrong expectations, bad customers, refund pressure, security risk, brand dilution, and support burden.

### 2. Outcome-Based Partnership

Partners must not sell Ariyo as "AI magic".

Partners must sell specific outcomes:

- reduce support workload
- respond faster to leads
- centralize knowledge
- automate repetitive operations
- improve internal execution
- reduce manual workflow cost
- increase response consistency
- track AI employee ROI

### 3. Product-Governed Ecosystem

All partner actions must be governed through product, permission, attribution, and audit systems.

Partners must not operate outside Ariyo governance.

### 4. Plan-Aware Growth

Basic, Pro, and Smart must create different partner opportunities.

Partners must understand what each plan can and cannot deliver.

### 5. No Overclaiming

Partners must not promise guaranteed revenue, guaranteed AI accuracy, guaranteed AI citations, unlimited usage, impossible security, or full automation without human review.

## Plan-Aware Partner Positioning

Partners must explain Basic, Pro, and Smart clearly and honestly.

### Basic Partner Positioning

Basic is for customers who need a safe first AI employee experience with controlled usage and limited automation.

Partner message:

> Start with one focused AI employee, limited knowledge, clear controls, and low-risk adoption.

Best for:

- small teams
- first AI employee experiment
- basic support assistant
- simple lead response
- small knowledge base
- low-volume content assistant

Partner opportunity:

- fast setup
- light onboarding
- education
- referral conversion

Partners must not sell Basic as:

- full automation
- advanced workflow engine
- enterprise-ready deployment
- unlimited AI usage
- Smart-level reasoning

---

### Pro Partner Positioning

Pro is for teams using Ariyo operationally in daily business workflows.

Partner message:

> Deploy AI employees into real business processes with stronger workflow, integration, review, analytics, and cost controls.

Best for:

- growing companies
- customer support teams
- sales operations
- agencies
- internal operations teams
- content and knowledge teams

Partner opportunity:

- implementation services
- workflow setup
- integration setup
- training
- monthly optimization
- expansion into more AI employees

Partners must explain:

- Pro has more capabilities than Basic
- Pro still has controlled usage
- Pro is best for business operations
- Pro is not the full Smart automation layer

---

### Smart Partner Positioning

Smart is Ariyo's most advanced plan for customers that need deeper AI intelligence, richer workflows, higher automation, stronger analytics, more advanced integrations, and full business impact tracking.

Partner message:

> Use the full Ariyo operating system to run advanced AI employees with stronger reasoning, deeper knowledge, smarter workflows, richer integrations, predictive cost controls, and executive-level visibility.

Best for:

- multi-team organizations
- high-volume support
- sales and support automation
- advanced workflow automation
- multi-channel operations
- AI transformation projects
- teams needing higher intelligence and governance

Partner opportunity:

- strategic implementation
- high-ticket consulting
- ongoing optimization retainer
- enterprise upgrade
- marketplace solutions
- custom workflow design
- executive reporting

Partners must explain:

- Smart is fuller and more powerful
- Smart costs more because it uses more advanced AI capability
- Smart still has budget, approval, security, and governance controls
- Smart is not unlimited, unmanaged, or risk-free

## Attribution Rules

Ariyo must prevent attribution confusion and commission disputes.

### Attribution Sources

- referral link
- referral code
- partner-submitted lead
- marketplace listing referral
- webinar registration
- campaign UTM
- co-branded landing page
- partner API event

### Attribution Priority

Recommended priority:

1. manually approved deal registration
2. partner-submitted lead accepted by Ariyo
3. first-touch referral link within attribution window
4. last-touch partner campaign within attribution window
5. marketplace template install attribution
6. organic/direct attribution

### Attribution Window

Default recommendation:

```text
Referral window: 90 days
Affiliate window: 30 days
Marketplace attribution: 180 days for template-driven activation
Enterprise deal registration: 180 days or custom agreement
```

Final values must be controlled in billing/partner settings, not hardcoded.
